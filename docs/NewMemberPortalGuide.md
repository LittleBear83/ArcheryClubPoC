# New Member Portal Guide

## Welcome

Welcome to the Selby Archers member portal. The portal is designed to help you
manage your membership details, see what is happening at the club, and book onto
club activities.

## Logging In

- Use your member username and password on the login page.
- If your club card is enabled, you may also be able to sign in using RFID.
- Guest sign-in is available separately for visiting archers and is normally
  completed with the help of the member who invited them.

## What You Will See First

After logging in, you will arrive at the **Home** page. This page gives you a
 quick view of:

- members currently at the range
- your upcoming club event bookings
- tournament reminders linked to your account

Use the **Menu** button in the banner to open the side menu and move around the
portal.

## Main Pages For Members

### Profile

Use the **Profile** page to review and update your personal details held in the
portal. This is the best place to keep your information current.

### Event / Competition Calendar

The **Event / Competition Calendar** shows club events and competitions. From
here, members can view upcoming activity and book onto eligible events.

### Coaching Calendar

The **Coaching Calendar** shows available coaching sessions. If a session has
space, you can book onto it through the portal.

### Tournaments

The **Tournaments** page shows current and upcoming tournaments. You can:

- check registration and score submission windows
- register for tournaments while registration is open
- withdraw during the registration window if needed
- submit scores during the score window when required

### Feedback Form

Use the **Feedback Form** to share comments about the club, the range, or the
portal.

### Ideas Form

Use the **Ideas Form** to suggest improvements, new activities, or ways to make
the club experience better.

### Lost And Found

Use the **Lost and Found** page to record or check items that have been found at
the range.

### Range Usage

The **Range Usage** page gives a view of attendance patterns at the range. This
can help members understand busy times and general usage trends.

## Pages You May Not See

Some pages are only shown to members with additional responsibilities, such as
club officers, committee members, coaches, or administrators. Examples include:

- User Creation
- Roles & Permissions
- Loan Bow Register
- Tournament Setup
- Committee Org Chart management features

If you do not see one of these pages, it usually means your account does not
need access to it.

## Good Practice

- Keep your profile details up to date.
- Check the Home page regularly for reminders.
- Register early for events and tournaments where places may be limited.
- Log out when you finish, especially on a shared device.

## Need Help?

If something looks wrong, if you cannot access a page you expect to see, or if
you are unsure how to use a feature, contact a club administrator or committee
member for support.
